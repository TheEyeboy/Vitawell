About Our Hospital

At Healthy Living, our mission is to empower people to take control of their well-being and live their healthiest lives. We provide accurate, science-backed information and resources on a wide range of health topics including nutrition, fitness, mental health, disease prevention, and more. Our team of experienced health professionals, including doctors, nutritionists, personal trainers, and mental health experts, are passionate about making holistic health knowledge accessible to all. We cut through the noise and fads to deliver trustworthy, up-to-date guidance rooted in extensive research and clinical expertise. We believe that optimal health is achieved through a balanced approach that addresses the whole self - mind, body, and spirit. Our content equips you with the knowledge and motivation to make sustainable lifestyle choices aligned with your individual needs and goals. Whether you're just beginning your journey to better health or are a seasoned wellness enthusiast, Healthy Living is here to inspire and support you every step of the way. Join our vibrant community committed to helping one another thrive. Together, we can make the journey towards maximum health more accessible and enjoyable for all.

Our Hospital services

Pulmonology Department

The Pulmonology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory diseases and conditions. Our team of pulmonologists, respiratory therapists, and sleep specialists offers comprehensive care for asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), pneumonia, lung cancer, and sleep disorders. We provide pulmonary function testing, bronchoscopy, respiratory therapy, and smoking cessation programs to improve lung health and quality of life.

Rheumatology Department

The Rheumatology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of autoimmune and musculoskeletal disorders. Our team of rheumatologists, physical therapists, and occupational therapists provides comprehensive care for conditions such as rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, osteoarthritis, fibromyalgia, and gout. We offer disease-modifying medications, pain management strategies, and rehabilitation services to improve joint function and quality of life for patients with rheumatic diseases.

Cardiology Department

The Cardiology Department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart-related conditions. Our dedicated team of cardiologists and healthcare professionals is committed to providing comprehensive cardiac care, including cardiac screenings, non-invasive procedures, interventional cardiology, and cardiac rehabilitation.

Neurology Department

The Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles. Our neurologists and healthcare professionals offer comprehensive care for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and other neurological disorders. We provide advanced diagnostic testing, treatment plans, and ongoing management to improve the quality of life for our patients.

Orthopedics Department

The Orthopedics Department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of musculoskeletal conditions and injuries. Our team of orthopedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and rehabilitation specialists provides comprehensive care for fractures, sports injuries, arthritis, spinal disorders, and joint replacements. We offer advanced surgical techniques, minimally invasive procedures, and personalized rehabilitation programs to help patients regain mobility and improve their quality of life.

Pediatrics Department

The Pediatrics Department specializes in providing healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team of pediatricians, nurses, and child specialists offers preventive care, vaccinations, routine check-ups, and treatment for various childhood illnesses and developmental issues. We are committed to promoting the health and well-being of our young patients and supporting families through every stage of childhood.

Gastroenterology Department

The Gastroenterology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders. Our team of gastroenterologists, endoscopists, and nutritionists provides comprehensive care for conditions such as gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD), inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), liver diseases, and pancreatic disorders. We offer advanced diagnostic procedures, therapeutic interventions, and dietary management to improve gastrointestinal health and quality of life.

Dermatology Department

The Dermatology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail disorders. Our team of dermatologists, dermatopathologists, and cosmetic specialists offers medical and surgical interventions for conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin cancer, and cosmetic concerns. We provide personalized care, dermatological screenings, and innovative therapies to promote healthy skin and enhance appearance.

Endocrinology Department

The Endocrinology Department specializes in the diagnosis and management of hormonal disorders and metabolic conditions. Our team of endocrinologists, diabetes educators, and nutritionists provides comprehensive care for diabetes, thyroid disorders, adrenal diseases, and hormonal imbalances. We offer personalized treatment plans, hormone replacement therapy, and lifestyle interventions to optimize hormonal health and improve overall well-being.
